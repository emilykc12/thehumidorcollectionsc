About Us

The Humidor Collection is an at-home local embroidery and sewing service, operated right from our unique boat-home in Charleston, SC. We offer everything from simple pocket tee embroidery to custom curtains for your home, with prices ranging from $10 to $300. Whether it's personalized apparel or bespoke upholstery, we bring a nautical flair and modern creativity to every project.

Our Services

  • Pocket Tees: Fun, custom embroidery on tees for that personal splash!
  • Polos: Sharp designs on polos with a beachy vibe.
  • Bags & Hats: Embroider your gear with unique nautical motifs.
  • Any Type of Clothing: From jackets to jeans, we customize with flair.
  • Custom Upholstery: Tailored curtains, cushions, and more – perfect for homes or boats!
